About

Stephen Mar is an entrepreneur and investor with deep conviction in the transformative power of crypto and Web3 technologies.

He backs founders and protocols where asymmetric opportunities are deliberately engineered — not hoped for. Markets are his first language; long-term edge is the only metric that matters.

With a sharp, no-nonsense approach, he focuses on structural resilience, narrative velocity, and the quiet compounding of high-conviction positions in an evolving digital economy.
